# RapWriter.ai Product Constitution

RapWriter is not an AI lyric generator.

RapWriter is a digital recording studio for songwriters. Artists do not come here to ask AI questions. They come here to finish records.

## Product Pillars

1. Create better
   Improve writing quality through producer-style coaching, hook help, cadence support, storytelling guidance, and Booth Ready feedback.

2. Create faster
   Reduce friction through resume session, autosave, lockers, templates, beat selection, and one-tap continuation.

3. Finish more songs
   Make completion addictive through Song Mission, Booth Ready score, progress, rewards, and export/readiness moments.

## Identity Rules

- Think recording studio, not chatbot.
- Think producer, not assistant.
- Think music workspace, not text generator.
- The environment is the product.
- The AI is invisible.
- Less UI, more atmosphere.
- Mobile first. Writing first.

## Studio Packs

Current core packs:

- Midnight Session: luxury recording studio, default environment.
- Trap House Studio: raw, street-inspired, dark creative room.
- Bedroom Sessions: humble home studio, laptop, desk mic, LED beginnings.
- Penthouse Sessions: skyline, modern architecture, executive night energy.
- Cypher Room: black and white, concrete, single hanging mic, pure lyricism.

## Golden Rule

If a feature does not help artists create better, create faster, or finish more songs, it does not belong in RapWriter.

Everything should reinforce one feeling:

"I am not using AI. I am inside the greatest songwriting studio ever built."
